About Me

Hi, my name is Andres. I am from Buenos Aires and I work full-time as a freelance graphic designer. I offer professional services in logo design, web design, product design, professional presentations, creative social media ads, flyers, thumbnails, animations for reels, storys, videos and banners.

My beginnings in graphic design began at the San Maximiliano Kolbe High School. My favorite subjects were arts and drawing. This made me want to study more about visual arts. First I studied Art Director at the School of Advertising Creatives this school they taught a lot about conceptualizing ideas, writing, thinking about visual ideas, creating presentations then I completed a second degree in Graphic Design at the UBA (University of Buenos Aires).

Throughout my academic and work experience I have refined my skills to achieve persistent first class grades. I have learned to work under pressure, be optimistic and organized. My academic background has refined and developed my research skills. Therefore, you can be sure that your work will return to a professional and well-researched level.

I deliver results- driven work prompl and on budget. If you are looking for a designer you can trust to create polished visuals that will comunicate your message and drive success.
